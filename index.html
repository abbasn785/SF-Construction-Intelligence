SF Construction Intelligence

Weekly San Francisco construction lead reports for subcontractors, suppliers, and service providers.

What We Do

SF Construction Intelligence is a paid digital information service that analyzes recent San Francisco building permit activity and turns it into a concise weekly lead report. The goal is to help construction-related businesses identify projects that may need bidding, materials, labor, or specialty services.

What Customers Receive

  • A weekly digital report delivered by email
  • A curated list of active San Francisco construction projects based on public permit data
  • Project summaries, estimated values, and permit descriptions
  • Relevant business/contact information when available
  • A concise format designed for sales outreach and lead generation

Who It’s For

This service is intended for subcontractors, suppliers, estimators, sales teams, and other construction industry professionals looking for new project opportunities in San Francisco.

Delivery

This is a subscription-based digital product. Subscribers are billed on a recurring basis and receive their report electronically. No physical goods are shipped.
